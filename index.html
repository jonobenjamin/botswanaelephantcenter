About the Centre

The Botswana Elephant Centre is a space dedicated to understanding and celebrating elephants, bringing together science, education, and community engagement.

Why Elephants Matter

Botswana is home to the largest population of elephants in Africa. Protecting them means protecting ecosystems, livelihoods, and biodiversity.
